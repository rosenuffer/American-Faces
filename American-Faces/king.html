Martin Luther King Jr.

Martin Luther King Jr., a prominent leader of the Civil Rights Movement, dedicated his life to fighting racial injustice and advocating for equality. Through nonviolent protests and powerful speeches, such as the famous I Have a Dream speech or the less famous Beyond Vietnam, he inspired millions and brought attention to the struggle for civil rights in America. His legacy continues to inspire generations, reminding us of the ongoing quest for justice and the transformative power of peaceful activism.
