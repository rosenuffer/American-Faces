Dolores Huerta

Dedicating her life to advocating for the rights of farmworkers and marginalized communities, Dolores Huerta fought to make America a better place for its common people. As a co-founder of the United Farm Workers and a relentless organizer, she led strikes, fought for fair wages, and championed women's rights. Huerta's unwavering commitment and remarkable achievements continue to inspire and remind us of the power of collective action in creating a more just and equitable world.
